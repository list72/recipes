Fried potatoes

How to make fried potatoes

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 24 min

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes to desired size.
  2. Cut the onions
  3. Put a bit of Oil in your pan.
  4. Put both the potatoes and the onions into the pan.
  5. Cook them until they are golden-brown.
